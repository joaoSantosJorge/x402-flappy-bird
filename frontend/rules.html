üéÆ How to Play

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Connect using MetaMask, WalletConnect, or Coinbase Wallet to participate
  2. Purchase Tries: Pay 0.02 USDC to receive 10 game tries
  3. Play & Score: Navigate the square through pipes to achieve the highest score possible
  4. Compete: Your best score is automatically submitted to the monthly leaderboard
  5. Win Prizes: Top players share the prize pool at the end of each cycle

‚è∞ Competition Cycles

Cycle Duration

Each competition cycle runs for a configurable period (typically 30 days). The cycle timer is displayed in the top-left corner of the game.

Cycle Phases

  • Active Phase: Players compete and scores are recorded on the leaderboard
  • Allocation Phase: Admin allocates funds to winners based on final rankings
  • Claim Phase: Winners have 7 days to claim their rewards
  • Reset Phase: New cycle begins with a fresh leaderboard and prize pool

üí∞ Prize Pool Distribution

Pool Accumulation

The prize pool grows from:

  • Player entry fees (0.02 USDC per 10 tries)
  • Direct donations to the contract
  • Unclaimed rewards from previous cycles (if not claimed within 7 days)

Winner Allocation

At the end of each cycle, the admin allocates the prize pool to the top players. The allocation structure is configurable but typically follows this pattern:

Rank Allocation % Example (100 USDC Pool)
1st Place 40% 40 USDC
2nd Place 25% 25 USDC
3rd Place 15% 15 USDC

5th Place 5% 5 USDC

Flexible Allocation

The admin can configure:

  • Number of winners (1-10 positions)
  • Percentage allocation for each position
  • Admin fee percentage
  • All percentages must total exactly 100%

üèÜ Claiming Rewards

  • Winners can claim rewards by clicking the "Claim Reward" button
  • Rewards are sent directly to your connected wallet
  • You have 7 days after allocation to claim your reward
  • Unclaimed rewards after 7 days return to the prize pool
  • No transaction fees - you receive the full allocated amount

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Rules

  • Fair Play: Scores are validated server-side to prevent cheating
  • One Wallet, One Entry: Each wallet address gets one entry on the leaderboard (highest score)
  • Monthly Reset: Leaderboard resets at the end of each cycle
  • Smart Contract: All funds are held and distributed via a transparent smart contract on Base network
  • No Refunds: Entry fees are non-refundable once paid
  • Admin Controls: Admin can adjust cycle timing, winner count, and allocation percentages

Smart Contract

  • Server-side validation prevents score manipulation
  • Invalid scores are automatically rejected

‚ùì FAQ

Can I play without connecting a wallet?

You can play in practice mode, but to compete on the leaderboard and win prizes, you must connect a wallet and pay the entry fee.

What happens if there's a tie?

Rankings are determined by score and timestamp. If two players have the same score, the player who achieved it first ranks higher.

Can I submit multiple scores?

Yes! You can play as many times as you have tries. Only your highest score appears on the leaderboard.

How do I know when the cycle ends?

The countdown timer is displayed in the top-left corner of the game screen.

Is this game provably fair?

Yes! All prize distributions happen through a public smart contract on the blockchain. You can verify all transactions and allocations.
